About us

Our Journey


We began with a simple idea: no food should go to waste, and everyone deserves a homemade meal.

Starting locally, we connected home chefs with foodies in their neighborhoods. What began as a small community project has grown into a platform where extra meals find hungry plates, reducing waste and spreading happiness.

Our journey is about building trust, sharing care, and making homemade food accessible to all.



Transforming Homemade 

Food Sharing


With trust, care, and community at the heart, we make homemade meals accessible to everyone. Our platform empowers home chefs to share their extra or freshly prepared meals, while giving food lovers the chance to enjoy authentic, affordable, and home-style dishes.

We go beyond just delivery — we build connections between kitchens and customers. Every dish listed is picked up safely by our riders and delivered fresh to your doorstep, so you can enjoy meals that feel like home.

By reducing food waste, we not only create value for home chefs and customers but also contribute to a sustainable future where food is respected, shared, and celebrated.
